OpenPLC and FactoryIO, PLC automation: Warehouse

TAGS: electronics; automation; industrial; PLC; OpenPLC; FactoryIO; Modbus

Why I Did This

In previous articles, I've mentioned training and experiencing PLC development using OpenPLC and FactoryIO. In this demo, I using SFC to program FactoryIO's warehouse scene.

FactoryIO's 'Warehouse' scene, programmed through OpenPLC.

Highlights

In this scene, I use SFC language to program the warehouse scene. The scene has a grid of pallet storage spots (the warehouse storage), along with loading and unloading conveyors and a forklift that can extend in opposite directions and reach every grid position.
